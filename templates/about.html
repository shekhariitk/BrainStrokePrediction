About Stroke Risk Predictor

What is this tool?

The Stroke Risk Predictor is a machine learning-based application that estimates your risk of having a stroke based on various health and lifestyle factors. It analyzes your input data and provides a probability score indicating your stroke risk level.

Important Note

This tool is for informational purposes only and does not constitute medical advice. Always consult with a healthcare professional for medical concerns.

Understanding Stroke

A stroke occurs when the blood supply to part of your brain is interrupted or reduced, preventing brain tissue from getting oxygen and nutrients. Brain cells begin to die in minutes.

Stroke Risk Factors
  • High blood pressure
  • Heart disease
  • Diabetes
  • Smoking
  • High cholesterol
  • Physical inactivity
  • Obesity
Prevention Tips
  • Control blood pressure
  • Exercise regularly
  • Eat a healthy diet
  • Maintain healthy weight
  • Limit alcohol
  • Don't smoke
  • Manage stress

How It Works

Our prediction model uses machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of medical records to identify patterns associated with stroke risk. The model considers:

Demographics
Age, gender, marital status
Health Metrics
BMI, glucose levels
Medical History
Hypertension, heart disease
Lifestyle Factors
Smoking status, work type

Model Accuracy

87% Accuracy

Our model achieves 87% accuracy in predicting stroke risk based on testing with clinical datasets. This means it correctly identifies stroke risk in 87 out of 100 cases.
